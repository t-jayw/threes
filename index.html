The game is played as follows:
To start the game, the player rolls 6 dice. The player must select at least one die per roll. The player, after selecting their die or dice from the previous roll, then rolls the remaining, unselected, dice. This is repeated until all dice have been selected and none are left to roll. The player's score is the sum of all selected dice after all 6 have been chosen, with the exception of THREES which are worth 0 points. The aim is to end with as low of a score as possible.
